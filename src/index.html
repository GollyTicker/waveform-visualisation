Waveform visualiser

  1. Open this page in an up to date Google Chrome browser.
  2. Upload your audio file
  3. Click once on Conversion step to start. The audio will playback and the visualisation will be drawn and recorded.
  4. When you reach the end of your audio, click on Conversion step again to stop the playback. The recorded visualisation will be converted to a video.
  5. When the conversion is finished, the video will automatically play the recording. (the conversion can take a while for long audio files.)
  6. Finally you can right click on the video and click "Save video as..." to save it.
